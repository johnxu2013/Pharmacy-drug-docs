Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

Introduction

Pharmacy Drug ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our iOS application.

Data Collection

Pharmacy Drug is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All data is stored locally on your device.

Information Stored Locally

  • Your saved medication list
  • Medication reminders and schedules
  • Favorite drugs and searches
  • App preferences and settings

💡 Important: This data never leaves your device and is protected by iOS's built-in security features and encryption.

Third-Party Services

OpenFDA API

We use the openFDA API to provide drug information and recall data. This is a public government service that does not require authentication. When you search for drugs or recalls, your query is sent to openFDA's servers, but no personal information is transmitted.

Apple Maps

We use Apple's MapKit framework to help you find nearby pharmacies. Location queries are handled by Apple according to their privacy policy. We do not collect or store your location data.

Permissions

Location Services

When you use the "Find Nearby Pharmacies" feature, we request access to your location. This permission is optional and only used when you explicitly search for nearby pharmacies. Your location is never stored or transmitted to us.

Notifications

We request permission to send notifications for medication reminders. These are local notifications generated on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData, Apple's modern data persistence framework. This data is protected by:

  • iOS device encryption
  • App sandboxing
  • Secure storage in iOS's protected data directory

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from users. It can be safely used by individuals of all ages under appropriate supervision.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by:

  • Deleting the app from your device
  • Using iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Pharmacy Drug → Delete App

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
